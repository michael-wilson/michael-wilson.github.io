A Virtual Tour of the Hounsfield Facility

As we're locked down and living a virtual existence, no one can currently visit the Hounsfield Facility at The University of Nottingham, which was to be the showcase visit of the phenotyping session at Rooting2020. To make up and in the spirit of the times we've made taken it online and with the help of Dr Robyn Ward and Dr Faraz Khan from our Digital Research Team we have created a virtual tour of the Facility. Click the button below to start the tour and engage with the videos placed around the building to discover what happens there and explore its cutting edge capabilities.
